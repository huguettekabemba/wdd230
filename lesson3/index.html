Website Planning Assignment

Site Name

South Africa - DR Congo
Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the South Africa - DR Congo Chamber of Commerce is to emphasize the importance for companies and individuals in understanding the potential benefits and opportunities that are open to South African business people in the DRC and to Congolese business people seeking to conduct business in South Africa.

To enhance the ongoing efforts of both countries to forge partnerships and projects promoting development and sustainability.

To promote reciprocal trade between the DRC and South Africa by providing a range of services and activities designed to facilitate business opportunities within the South African and Congolese business communities.

To provide our members with first hand business information, opportunities and appropriate links with South African or Congolese partners in each market.

The southafricadrcongochamber.com website will be used to help it members to meet, communicate and discuss business issues and opportunities.
