Target Audience Description

The target audience of the southafricadrcongochamber.com website:

Who should be a member of the SA-DRC Chamber: Organizations and Individuals seeking investment, trade from private / public sector partnerships; Companies / Institutions that are involved in Agriculture, Transport, Energy, ICT, Water and Sanitation, Healthcare, Education. Importers / Exporters looking to grow their businesses in the DRC or South Africa.

Technology: Our audience primarily uses their cellular devices to access information. The second most common is tablets and computers.
